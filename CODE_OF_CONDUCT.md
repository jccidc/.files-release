# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and respectful environment for everyone who interacts with this project, regardless of experience level, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, personal appearance, race, ethnicity, age, religion, or nationality.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior includes:**
- Being respectful and constructive in feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy toward others

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to **jimmy@jccidc.com**. All reports will be reviewed and may result in a response deemed appropriate to the circumstances, including a temporary or permanent ban from interaction.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, discussions, and any public space where an individual is representing the project.
